Help

Progress, Levels and Tiers


The progress bar automatically earns progress. Once it is full, it will empty and both a level and a tier will be earned. Progress can be speeded up for some time by clicking the click button. Levels can be spent on 5 level upgrades that increase the progress earned.

Reset


Several things can be reset (Levels, level upgrades, etc.) to increase the reset multi, which increases the progress earned. After clicking the reset button, there will be further explanation.

Energy

Power

Reversed Generator

The Reversed Generator is unlocked at 20k tiers. Energy can be converted to a temporary progress boost. RG tokens can be used to get the highest previously used boost for 180 seconds, they can be stacked.

Reward Road

The Reward Road is a battle pass-like feature unlocked at 30k tiers. Reward Road Progress can be earned from several things. Further explanation at the Reward Road. The collection contains several trophies, which can be earned from codes and at the Reward Road. They are decoration.

Quests

Quests are unlocked at 50k tiers. There's 5 quest slots, new quests will appear when saving. Each quest has a randomly selected thing that has to be done and a randomly selected reward of the same difficulty.

Common Abbreviations

Some common abbreviations you may find in the patch notes or community:
RG = Reversed Generator
RR = Reward Road
RRP = Reward Road Progress
IB = Idle Bar
LLTS = Lowlevel Triplespeed
